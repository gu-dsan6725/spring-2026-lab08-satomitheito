### 1. Comcast Internet - Current: $89.99/month

#### Market Research
**Competitor Prices:**
- Verizon Fios: $49.99/month
- AT&T Fiber: $54.99/month

**Leverage Points:**
- Long-term customer loyalty
- Competitive offers significantly lower
- Willingness to cancel

#### Negotiation Script

**When to call:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10 AM–2 PM
**Ask for:** Retention Department

**Opening Statement:**
> "Hi, I've been a loyal Comcast customer for [X years] and my bill has risen to $89.99/month. I've received quotes from Verizon Fios at $49.99 and AT&T Fiber at $54.99 for comparable speeds. Before I switch, I wanted to see if Comcast can offer a loyalty rate. What promotions do you have available?"

**Present Alternatives:**
> "I have written quotes from two competitors at $49.99 and $54.99 per month for comparable service."

**Target Price:** $64.99/month (down from $89.99)
**Acceptable Range:** $59.99 - $69.99/month

**Handle Objections:**

**If they say:** "I can offer you $79.99/month"
**You respond:** "I appreciate that, but it's still much higher than $49.99 from competitors. Can you get to $69.99 or below?"

**If they say:** "That's the best we can do"
**You respond:** "Can you process my cancellation for 30 days from now?" (Often triggers a better offer immediately)

**Closing:**
- **If accepted:** "Thank you. Can you confirm this rate in writing via email? And how long is this promotional rate locked in for?"
- **If rejected:** "Please process my cancellation. I'll switch to Verizon Fios."

#### Expected Savings
- **Monthly:** $25.00
- **Annual:** $300.00
- **Success Probability:** 85%
